Who is Taylor Swift?

Taylor Swift, (born December 13, 1989), is an American singer-songwriter. Her discography spans across a variety of genres, specifically pop and country, and her songs are world renowned. Taylor released her self-titled first album in 2006, debuting number 19 on the Billboard Hot 100. Since then, Taylor has released 8 more albums, as well as two rerecordings of her previous albums, and her success has reached unmatched levels. Her albums have consistently topped the charts, and she has collected 11 Grammys throughout her career. Taylor's lyrics and music continues to inspire and gain critical praise to this day, and her success only grows with each new album and rerecording she releases.
