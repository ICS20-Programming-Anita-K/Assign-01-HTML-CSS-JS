Folklore Deep Dive



Folklore is the eighth studio album by Taylor Swift, and was dropped as a surprise release on July 24, 2020. This was not just another album however, it was more of a lyrical work of art. Folklore marked a change in era and in tone for Taylor, with the album having a much more tender and indie feel in comparison to her other seven albums. The exiquisitely complex, poetic, and beautiful lyricism of this album are a stand-out factor of Folklore, as well as the storytelling components. Many of the songs in the album are interconnected and tell the same story of a love-triangle spread across three songs: "Cardigan", "August", and "Betty", with each song telling a different character's point of view. Folklore and its lyrics connected with fans and casual listeners alike, and these factors are what make it my favourite Taylor Swift album.
